Georgina Walton MA, BA Hons., Dip Sc.

Georgina left school to join the Women’s Royal Naval Service. After seven years service she became a teacher, firstly of art, later of physics and science. She has worked with various art media throughout her life.

While painting in oils and then acrylics has been a dominant theme, she also worked for twelve years with ceramics. Her regular contributions to Hampshire exhibitions meet with considerable success, and she passes on her expertise during art workshops for adults.

She found painting illustrations for The Naturals children’s stories was a new and thoroughly enjoyable experience, adding a further dimension to her art skills.
